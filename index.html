Look-Ahead Conserving EDF

Algorithm Brief

The scheduler expands periodic tasks into jobs, chooses ready work by earliest absolute deadline, reserves future WCET demand conservatively, and drops to the lowest feasible P-state when slack exists. When actual execution finishes early, unused WCET reservation becomes reclaimed slack.
