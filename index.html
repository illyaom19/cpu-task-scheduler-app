LOOK-AHEAD_CONSERVING_EDF_TERMINAL

Algorithm Brief
  • Which: ready jobs run by earliest absolute deadline.
  • When: each periodic release becomes a job that can run until it completes or reaches its deadline.
  • Why: look-ahead reserves WCET demand, then uses the lowest P-state that can still protect future deadlines.
  • Misses: a job misses when actual execution remains at its deadline; playback calls out that moment in red.
