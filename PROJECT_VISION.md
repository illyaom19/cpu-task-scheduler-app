The year is 2077 and We just got out of a car chase of our lifetime. We made it through but the car needs work. We take it to the finest mechanics in the city - droid mechanics. They are dedicated and straight to the point, and their motors are always pristine and their imaging sensors are exquisite. I catch a peek at one of their car diagnostic terminals, and find them looking at a CPU task scheduling tool that they are using to reprogram the ECU. It is exquisitely crafted, with monospaced fonts, and a purpose-driven heavily practical design. Everything is where it should be and it's made to be incredibly easy and intuitive for a human to learn and understand, and a robot to master. This is the design philosophy of this project.

The project should use a dark theme. Highlighted items are orange, and the accent thematics are shades of vibrant orange, and shades of dark grey. UI elements should be custom, sliders with clean gradients should be used for any input entries, with things like "Worst case" and "Actual Execution Time" being two ticks on one slider (a red and an orange). Animations are to be mixed with the rigidity of a terminal-looking design. This is a blend of modern excellence and archaic valor.
